How to make a tea:

At the very least, tea is a flavorful way of getting enough fluid into your boddy each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant-based (Optional)
  • Sugar or Honey (Optional)

Preoaration

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up.
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug(always a big mug)
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all its flavor, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its thing. This is a perfect time to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about vacations.
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon agaionst the side of te mug. Not too much or you'll make it bitter.
  6. If you want, throw in some milk, or sugar, or honey... or nothing else at all.
  7. Enjoy!
